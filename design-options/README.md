# Design Options for Chicago Comedy Calendar

## Option 1: Dark Mode Comedy Club 🌃

**Vibe:** Sophisticated nightclub with neon accents

**Color Palette:**
- Deep dark blues and blacks (#0A0E27, #1A1F3A)
- Hot pink/red neon (#FF2D55)
- Electric cyan (#00D9FF)
- Golden yellow accent (#FFD60A)

**Key Features:**
- Glowing neon effects and shadows
- Dark background with bright accents
- Sleek, modern feel
- RGB gradient borders
- Perfect for evening viewing

**Best For:** Users who prefer dark mode, modern aesthetic, nightlife vibe

---

## Option 2: Minimalist Chicago 🏙️

**Vibe:** Clean, professional, newspaper-inspired

**Color Palette:**
- Classic navy (#003E7E)
- Chicago flag red (#C8102E)
- Black and white (#1A1A1A, #FFFFFF)
- Light grays (#F0F0F0)

**Key Features:**
- Bold borders and sharp edges
- Brutalist design elements
- High contrast
- Chicago flag-inspired colors
- Drop shadows instead of soft shadows
- Swiss design influence

**Best For:** Users who want clarity, simplicity, professional look

---

## Option 3: Retro Comedy Poster 🎪

**Vibe:** Vintage theater marquee and circus posters

**Color Palette:**
- Classic red (#E63946)
- Cream/beige (#F8F4E8)
- Gold (#FFD700)
- Deep navy (#1D3557)

**Key Features:**
- Bold borders and heavy shadows
- Star decorations
- Striped accents
- Vintage typography style
- Textured background
- 1950s theater aesthetic

**Best For:** Users who love retro/vintage vibes, theatrical atmosphere

---

## How to Apply

To switch designs, simply replace the content of `styles.css` with one of these options:

1. `dark-club.css` - Option 1
2. `minimalist-chicago.css` - Option 2
3. `retro-poster.css` - Option 3

## Mix & Match

You can also combine elements:
- Use Dark Club colors with Minimalist layout
- Use Retro borders with Minimalist colors
- Use Dark Club effects with Retro typography
